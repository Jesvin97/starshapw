## üõ†Ô∏è Detailed Feature Audit (Massive Scope Delivered)

### 1. The Financial Core (Banking & Accounts)
*This module alone replaces standalone accounting software.*
*   **Multi-Bank Architecture**: Logic built specifically for **Indian Bank** & **HDFC Bank** transactions.
*   **Partner Ledger System**: Dedicated accounts for partners (e.g., **Abdul Vahab**), tracking every rupee invested or drawn.
*   **Cash Flow Intelligence**: Real-time dashboard of Inflows vs. Outflows across all channels (Cash, UPI, Bank).
*   **Drawings & Investments**: Separate tracking for owner withdrawals and capital injections.
*   **Daily Financial Reports**: Auto-generated PDF reports summarizing the day's cash position.

### 2. Operations & Sales Command Center
*Streamlining the "Chaos" of the Service Floor.*
*   **Job Card <-> Invoice Sync**: One-click conversion of a Job Card into a Final Invoice. No double entry.
*   **Advance Payment Handling**: Complex logic to handle partial advances, remaining balances, and final settlements.
*   **Service Ticket Lifecycle**: `Reported` -> `Triaged` -> `In Progress` -> `Completed` workflow.
*   **Inter-Branch Transfers**: Capability to transfer Job Cards between locations seamlessly.
*   **Professional PDF engine**:
    *   **Tax Invoices**: GST-compliant, branded invoice generation.
    *   **Warranty Certificates**: Dedicated separate legal certificate generation.
    *   **Job Cards**: "Customer Copy" (Acknowledgement) and "internal Copy" (Technician).

### 3. Smart Inventory & Procurement
*Managing the Supply Chain.*
*   **Purchase Order (PO) System**: Professional generation of POs for suppliers.
*   **Purchase Returns**: Structured workflow for returning defective items (Critical for warranty claims).
*   **HSN Code Automation**: Pre-configured HSN codes for tax compliance on every part.
*   **Asset Categorization**: Separation of 'Resale Products' vs 'Capital Assets' vs 'Internal Use Tools'.

### 4. Technical Excellence (Invisible Value)
*   **Cloud-Native Desktop App**: Built on **Electron**. Combines the smooth experience of a desktop app with the security of a cloud database.
*   **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: secure logins for Admin, Manager, and Floor Staff. Security is baked in.
*   **Training & Onboarding**: Hands-on sessions to teach the staff how to use the modern system.
